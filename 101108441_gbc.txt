## GBC stands for George brown College ##
George Brown College is a public college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a wide range of programs, including diplomas, degrees, and certificates, in various fields like business, technology, health sciences, 
and arts.  The college has multiple campuses across Toronto and is known for its hands-on approach to education and strong industry connections.  
It's a diverse and welcoming community with a focus on student success.
